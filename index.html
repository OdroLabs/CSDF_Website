Who We Are

Community-led support grounded in dignity and trust

Community Strength Development Foundation (CSDF) is a non-profit, voluntary organization registered with the Department of Social Services and inaugurated in 2002. Working independently — irrespective of race, religion, caste, and party politics — CSDF focuses on marginalized women in Sri Lanka, providing the support services needed to uplift their lives with dignity, respect, and equal opportunity.
