Services

Responsive services that meet women where they are

CSDF combines healthcare, prevention, counselling, legal support, and training into one connected support ecosystem.

Service Model

Peer-informed outreach, referral networks, practical follow-up, and community education designed for trust and continuity.

❤️

Health Services

Referrals, wellness guidance, and care navigation tailored to individual needs.

🩺

HIV Prevention

Prevention education, testing support, and linkage to treatment and follow-up.

📘

STI Education

Practical sexual health education and confidential support for informed decisions.

💬

Mental Health

Counselling, psychosocial support, and peer encouragement for emotional wellbeing.

🛡

Violence Prevention

Safety planning, crisis guidance, and pathways to urgent help when needed.

⚖

Legal Support

Know-your-rights sessions, referrals, and case navigation assistance.

🎓

Training Programs

Leadership, life skills, and income-support training for long-term empowerment.

🌍

Community Outreach

Field outreach that brings support, referrals, and education directly into communities.

How Support Works

Simple, practical, and confidential

1. Connect

Reach out through outreach teams, contact channels, or referrals.

2. Assess

Identify immediate health, legal, or social support priorities.

3. Support

Coordinate referrals, counselling, education, and follow-up.

Why It Works

Built around real barriers and realities

  • Confidential and trauma-aware engagement
  • Peer-led outreach that increases trust
  • Cross-referrals across health, rights, and support services
  • Ongoing follow-up instead of one-time interactions
